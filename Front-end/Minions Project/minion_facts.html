Minion facts

We all love these cute, pill-shaped, little banana and apple loving evil lab assistants, though truth be told, these little creatures are far from being evil. They are capable of caring (remember Agness unicorn toy?), and showing affection. (Bedtime kiss from Gru!)

They also have real-life names, such as, Dave, Mark, Jerry, Stuart, Bob, Phil, Tim and Jorge.

Minion History

It is said that the Minions have existed since the beginning of time as yellow henchmen. They serve the historys most ambitious evil villains. Through their lovable ineptitude, Minions have managed to thwart and ultimately destroy all of those bad guys.

TRIVIA: Did you know that the language Minion-ese was especially created for Despicable Me Minions? Some of the words are derived from Spanish, Japanese and even Korean words.

Evil Minion

In Despicable Movie 2 we are introduced to a new minion called the evil minion which is purple in color. They have frizzled hair with misshapen teeth, though they also share the love for banana and mischief.

They are basically minions injected with the mutagen PX-41 which turns them into an indestructible killing machine.

Spin-off movie

Because of their extreme popularity, even outshining the actors (Steve Carell) themselves, Illumination Entertainment had announced that a spin-off movie, featuring the Minions will be coming out around 2014.
